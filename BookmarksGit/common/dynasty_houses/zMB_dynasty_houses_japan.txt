#Japan Cadet Branches

#I generally connected a lot of clans via cadets to the major clans mainly since I hate a bunch of disconnected families and I like to properly use the cadet branch system CK3 has and it makes dynasty renown properly filled since I make it harder to get. To make the dynasty trees still usable I broke up things where it made sense. For example instead of making a super Fujiwara dynasty that has 200 cadets I made the base family be the various branches (Hokke, Nanke, etc.).
