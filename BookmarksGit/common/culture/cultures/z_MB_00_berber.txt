It does seem wrong that I have Germany, for example, broken into over 10 cultures in 1337 while the Berber tribes are all lumped together into super cultures that go from the Atlantic to the Siwa Oasis. There's also an aspect of questioning lumping all the tribes together like that due to Arabic sources. It'd be like lumping all the slavic cultures into 'slavic' in earlier dates just since some German missionaries didn't care about tribal differences
